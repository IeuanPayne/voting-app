Vote for Your Values!

See which parties are promising on the things that matter to us.

All the statements link to the seven areas of “wellbeing” defined in Welsh law, the Wellbeing of Future Genrations Act. Whatever party wins the election, their government is legally bound by this law.

Strong green economy
Strong communities
Good health
Climate and nature restoration
Thriving language and culture
Equality and an end to discrimination
Positive contribution to international affairs

Every statement is written by or extracted from the website of a non-governmental organisation that leads national action on the subject. Select the areas that mean the most to you. Then see what the party manifestos say in each of these areas. [The work of matching the statements below with manifesto commitments will be done with the Office of the Wellbeing Commissioner.]

Choose your favourite manifesto promise and then you will see which party said what. That’s it!
